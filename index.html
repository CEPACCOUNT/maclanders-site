Matthew Maclanders is a commercial real estate broker with KW Commercial, based in Greensboro and serving the Piedmont Triad region of North Carolina. His practice is built on three things: institutional-grade analysis, responsive communication, and a deep local network that gets deals closed.

Over 16 years in the business, Matt has worked with private investors, family offices, owner-users, and regional operators — advising on everything from value-add multifamily acquisitions to single-tenant retail dispositions, owner-user industrial buildings, and professional office leasing. He brings the rigor of a capital-markets underwriter to small and mid-sized Triad deals that too often get handled like residential transactions.

Clients work with Matt because he treats every listing and every buyer rep engagement like it matters — because it does. Whether you're underwriting your first value-add play or disposing of a long-held asset, you'll get the same preparation, the same candid advice, and the same direct line.

Market Coverage

The Piedmont Triad is my backyard.

Every submarket, every major corridor, every off-market relationship — built over 16 years on the ground.

Greensboro

The Triad's largest city and my home base. Active coverage across downtown office, corridor retail, and suburban multifamily — plus proximity to the Piedmont Triad International (PTI) logistics hub driving industrial demand.

Winston-Salem

A distinct capital-markets dynamic driven by Wake Forest Innovation Quarter, healthcare anchors, and deep-rooted institutional investment. Strong multifamily and medical office opportunity.

High Point

Furniture capital with a rapidly diversifying industrial and flex base. High Point Market gravity plus I-74/I-85 access make it a real logistics play — not just a furniture story.
